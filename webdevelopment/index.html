Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to buy domain and hosting separately?
Yes, domains and hosting services are client-side expenses. We assist you in selecting the most cost-effective hosting platforms and coordinate all domain mappings.
Can I edit the website content myself later?
Yes, we construct pages with clean, editable code structures and assist you in setting up simple templates if you need to update text or photos in the future.
What is basic vs advanced SEO setup?
Basic SEO configures page title, meta description, and semantic headings. Advanced SEO adds structured schema markups (JSON-LD), generates xml sitemaps, formats robots.txt exclusions, and integrates Google Analytics/Facebook pixels.
