Frequently Asked Questions

Does this fee cover the ad budget?
No, the ₹1,999/month fee covers campaign management, structuring, targeting, conversion API setup, daily monitoring, and reporting. The actual advertising budget is paid directly to Meta via your ad account.
Who creates the ad designs/creatives?
The client provides the ad creatives (images, copy, and videos). We advise you on the exact formats, lengths, and angles that work best based on our performance data.
What starting budget do you recommend?
We recommend starting with at least ₹300 - ₹500 per day to allow Facebook's machine learning algorithm to gather enough conversion data during the learning phase.
