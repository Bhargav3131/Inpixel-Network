Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the static vs carousel posts?
Static posts are single high-quality graphics or images, ideal for announcements or clean branding. Carousel posts contain up to 5 swipeable slides designed to tell a story, share tips, or present portfolio items, which helps increase time spent on your profile.
Do you handle video filming/Reels in this package?
This package focuses strictly on graphic static posts and carousels. For high-impact vertical video/Reels production, we recommend our specialized AI Generated Videos package.
How do we collaborate on the content?
We provide a draft content calendar for the entire month ahead of time. You get to review, comment, and request revisions on all graphics and captions before anything gets scheduled or published.
